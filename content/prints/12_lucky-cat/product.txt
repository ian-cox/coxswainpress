Description:

Two color, hand carved linoleum block print, printed on vintage Golding Platen Printing Press.

Kanji roughly translates to "10 million gold coins" and is traditionally a coin that Lucky Cat statues are holding. They are meant to bring good fortune and luck to the owner.
